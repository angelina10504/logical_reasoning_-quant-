README: Logical Reasoning Study Materials
This repository contains a comprehensive set of study guides, cheat sheets, and practice papers designed for logical reasoning and quantitative aptitude preparation.

📚 What's Inside
📖 Study Guides
Phase1_Study_Guide.pdf * Topics: Percentage, Profit & Loss, Average.

Concepts Covered: Multiplier approach, successive change formula, R% more/less, CP-SP-MP relationships, dishonest trader, weighted average, and error correction.

Practice: Includes 18 practice MCQs with solutions.

Phase2_Study_Guide.pdf * Topics: Numbers, Permutation & Combination, Probability.

Concepts Covered: Summation formulas, divisibility rules, factors, nPr/nCr, circular permutations, dictionary rank, addition/multiplication rules, cards/dice/coins, and P(at least one).

Practice: Includes 18 practice MCQs with solutions.

Phase3_Study_Guide.pdf * Topics: Work & Chain Rule, Series, Coding-Decoding.

Concepts Covered: Combined work, chain rule (MDH/W), pipes & cisterns, 10 number series patterns, alphabet series, and 5 coding types.

Practice: Includes 16 practice MCQs with solutions.
