# Logical Reasoning - Mid Term Exam Prep Kit

Complete study material for Logical Reasoning covering 9 topics: Numbers, Percentage, Profit & Loss, Average, Permutation & Combination, Probability, Work & Chain Rule, Series, and Coding-Decoding.

## What's inside

### Study Guides (Learn + Practice Together)

**`Phase1_Study_Guide.pdf`** — Percentage, Profit & Loss, Average. Covers multiplier approach, successive change formula, R% more/less, CP-SP-MP relationships, dishonest trader, weighted average, error correction. Includes 18 practice MCQs with solutions.

**`Phase2_Study_Guide.pdf`** — Numbers, Permutation & Combination, Probability. Covers summation formulas, divisibility rules, factors, nPr/nCr, circular permutations, dictionary rank, addition/multiplication rules, cards/dice/coins, P(at least one). Includes 18 practice MCQs with solutions.

**`Phase3_Study_Guide.pdf`** — Work & Chain Rule, Series, Coding-Decoding. Covers combined work, chain rule (MDH/W), pipes & cisterns, 10 number series patterns, alphabet series, 5 coding types. Includes 16 practice MCQs with solutions.

### Quick Reference

**`Logical_Reasoning_Cheat_Sheet.pdf`** — All key formulas across 9 topics on 5 pages. Quick revision before the exam.

### Practice Papers

**`Logical_Reasoning_MCQ_Paper.pdf`** — 25-mark practice paper (easy-moderate) with answer key and explanations.

**`Logical_Reasoning_Advanced_MCQ_Paper.pdf`** — 25-mark practice paper (moderate-hard, 1.5 hr exam level) with step-by-step solutions. Questions modeled after actual exam patterns.
